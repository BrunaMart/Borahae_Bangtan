About BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan / 방탄소년단 / Bulletproof Boy Scouts) is

a South Korean boy band formed in 2010. The group has seven

members:

  1. RM
  2. Suga
  3. J-Hope
  4. Jim
  5. V
  6. Jimin

who participate extensively in the creation of their own music,

helping to write and produce much of the material
