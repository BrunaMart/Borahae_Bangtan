What is ARMY?

ARMY (Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth) is the official name of the BTS

fandom. We are known for our strong support, creativity, and the positive

impact we create together.

ARMY Etiquette

  1. Stream music legally

  5. Use official hashtags when voting or streaming

Where to Follow BTS

Important Terms

  1. ARMY → Official fandom name
  2. Bias → Your favorite member
  3. Maknae → Youngest member (Jungkook)
  4. Hyung / Noona → How younger members call older ones
  5. I Purple You → BTS’s special way to say “I love you” (created by V)
