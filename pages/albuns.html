BTS Universe

The BTS Universe (BU) is a fictional storyline that connects several BTS albums. It tells the

story of seven friends who meet in school and create beautiful memories together. As they

grow up, each faces personal hardships that pull them apart. One member is given the

chance to travel back in time to save his friends from tragedy.


The story explores themes of youth, friendship, trauma, and self-love. It mainly spans the

HYYH, WINGS, Love Yourself, and Map of the Soul eras, told through music videos, short

films, and “The Notes” (diary entries).

In the end, the message is powerful: to truly love others and break the cycle of pain, you

must first learn to love yourself.

Chronological order of connected eras

Era/Series Period Main works Central theme What happens in the story?
HYYH (The Most Beautiful Moment in Life) 2015–2016 Pt.1, Pt.2, Young Forever + I Need U, Run Youth, friendship, pain, loss Introduces the characters living happy moments that slowly fall apart. It shows conflict, separation, accidents, and emotional struggles.
WINGS 2016 WINGS album + solo short films Temptation, guilt, growth, “wings” Each member confronts their inner darkness (inspired by Demian). Focuses on sin, choices, and personal growth through struggle.
Love Yourself 2017–2018 Her, Tear, Answer + Highlight Reels, Epiphany Self-love Jin tries to fix the timeline. The story shifts toward realizing that loving others starts with loving yourself. Emotional conclusion of pain → acceptance.
Map of the Soul 2019–2020 Persona, 7, One + Jungian concepts Identity, ego, shadow Based on Carl Jung’s psychology: persona (mask), shadow, and true self. Focuses on full self-acceptance and inner integration.
