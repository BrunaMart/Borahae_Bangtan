BT21

BT21 is a line of cute characters created in partnership with LINE Friends.

Launched in 2017, the project features eight characters that creatively

represent the BTS universe.


Each of the seven BTS members actively participated in the creation,

designing and defining the personalities of the characters, which reflect

traits of their own identity:

  1. KOYA (sleepy and thoughtful koala)
  2. RJ (gentle and foodie alpaca)
  3. SHOOKY (mischievous little cookie)
  4. MANG (dancing pony with a mask)
  5. CHIMMY (energetic and cheerful puppy)
  6. TATA (curious and romantic alien with a heart on his head)
  7. COOKY (strong and determined bunny)

Today, BT21 is one of LINE Friends most popular product lines, with thousands

of merchandise items (plush toys, clothing, accessories, etc.) and its own

animated series.
